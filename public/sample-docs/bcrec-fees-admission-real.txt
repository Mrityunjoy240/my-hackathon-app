B.Tech fee ranges from INR 1.09 to 1.69 Lakhs per year for the first year. Semester fee from the 2nd semester onwards is approximately INR 42,000. Hostel fee is INR 25,000 to 30,000 per semester including food. There are 5 hostels available, with 3 for boys and 2 for girls. Average fee for all courses is INR 93,920 per year. PG fee ranges from INR 69,000 to 2.25 Lakhs per year. B.Tech Admission is conducted via WBJEE for 80% seats and JEE Main for 10% seats via WBJEEB counseling. Management quota accounts for 10% of seats. Eligibility requires 10+2 PCM with 60% minimum. JEE Main cutoff 2024 rank ranged from 39,210 to 1,10,294. WBJEE cutoff 2024 rank ranged from 39,210 to 1,10,294. AI/ML JEE cutoff was 51,829 (Round 1, 2024). Lateral entry via JELET is available for diploma holders. Scholarships include merit scholarships for top students, SC/ST full fee waiver from the government, and need-based scholarships directly from management. Bank loans are easily sanctioned. Academic rules mandate 75% attendance. Internal assessments are 30 marks and external exams are 70 marks. The pass mark is 40% in each subject. Classes are held from 10 AM to 5:30 PM.
